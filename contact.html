Do you provide transportation facilities?

Yes, Mission Education Basic School provides safe and reliable vehicle facilities for our students. Our transportation service ensures comfortable and secure travel to and from school.

  • Safe Vehicles: Well-maintained buses and vans with experienced drivers
  • Route Coverage: Serving multiple areas around the school with convenient pickup and drop points
  • Safety Measures: All vehicles equipped with safety features and following traffic regulations
  • Attendant Support: Female attendants available in vehicles for student safety and assistance
  • Affordable Fees: Reasonable transportation charges with flexible payment options

For detailed information about routes, fees, and registration for transportation services, please contact our school office. We're committed to making the journey to school as safe and comfortable as the education we provide within our classrooms.

Are there any extracurricular activities?

Yes, at Mission Education Basic School we believe in holistic development beyond academics. We organize various extracurricular activities every Sunday to provide students with fun, engaging, and educational experiences outside the regular curriculum. Our Sunday activities include:

  • Fun Games & Sports: Outdoor and indoor games that promote physical fitness, teamwork, and friendly competition
  • Educational Movies: Carefully selected age-appropriate films that entertain while teaching valuable lessons
  • Quiz Competitions: Interactive quiz sessions covering general knowledge, academics, and current affairs to enhance learning
  • Creative Arts: Drawing, painting, and craft activities to nurture creativity and self-expression
  • Music & Dance: Opportunities for students to explore their musical and rhythmic talents
  • Storytelling Sessions: Interactive storytelling to improve language skills and imagination

These Sunday activities are designed to be both enjoyable and educational, helping students develop social skills, discover new interests, and create memorable school experiences in our natural campus environment. Participation is encouraged but optional, allowing students to explore activities that interest them most.
