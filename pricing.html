Our Commitment to Accessible Education

At Mission Education Basic School, we believe that financial circumstances should never be a barrier to quality education. Our flexible pricing model is designed with compassion to ensure that every child, regardless of economic background, has the opportunity to learn and grow.

We understand that each family has unique financial situations, and we're committed to working with parents to find a solution that makes quality education possible for their children.

Financial Assistance Programs

We offer various financial aid options to support families who need assistance with school fees.

Fee Waivers

Partial or complete waiver of admission and tuition fees based on family income and circumstances.

Flexible Payment Plans

Customized payment schedules that align with family income cycles and financial capabilities.

Scholarship Programs

Merit-based and need-based scholarships for exceptional students from underprivileged backgrounds.
